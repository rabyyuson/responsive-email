Tips for Success

Customers with the top performing campaigns on the network enjoy more traffic because they’ve covered off this optimization list. Make sure you do too!

Campaign Duration

Check your campaign is set to run for at least 2-3 weeks to give our system time to recommend your content on the network, build your audience and optimize the best performing pieces of content.

Test Titles

For each piece of content, you should test multiple titles so you can determine which headlines generate the most interest. Check out our how-to video for adding titles.  We’ve also listed our headline optimization posts to help you create the best performing titles for your campaign.

  1. How headline length impacts engagement
  2. The consequences of pushy headlines
  3. When the best brings the worst and the worst brings the best

Review Your CPC

When you signed up, we advised starting with our recommended CPC. Unless you have a large volume of content (think multiple RSS feeds with hundreds of posts), our recommended CPC is intended to help campaigns get enough reach and allow you to optimize your CPC as you go.

Create Additional Campaigns to Target Devices

The world has gone mobile so make sure you reach more of your audience across devices.  We’ve also listed our headline optimization posts to help you create the best performing titles for your campaign.

  1. Create a new campaign to target your audience on mobile

  2. If you have videos, add them to your campaign! If you’re yet to make video part of your content marketing plan, review our DOs and DON’Ts first!

Understanding our Content Guidelines

Our Content Guidelines have been designed to deliver only the best, most authentic, and most trustworthy content on the web. Make sure you review our guidelines to understand what content you can run in our network. And remember, adding fresh content regularly will help build your impressions and generate a new audience. The more content you have, the better!
