And if you’re data nerds like us and want to drill down further, we recommend appending a UTM tracking code to your campaign so you can see post-click engagement such as time spent on site and bounce rates for each piece of content in your campaign!! Keep Track of Your Campaign Spend

Once your campaign accrues the maximum amount of clicks for your campaign, it will turn off until the next day. If however, you don’t reach your daily budget maximum, you are only billed for the clicks you accrue. You’ll be sent an invoice on the 4th business day of each month and the credit card associated with your account will be charged on the following day. Learn more.
